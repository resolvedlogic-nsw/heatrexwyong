How it works

From spec to dispatch

01
Submit Spec

Send us your dimensions, voltage, wattage, and wire requirements — or let us reverse-engineer from a sample part.

02
R-Number Assigned

Your design is catalogued with a unique R-number. All drawings, specs, and job history are stored against it permanently.

03
Fabrication

Elements wound, assembled, and tested in-house. Each unit is checked for resistance and continuity before dispatch.

04
Reorder Anytime

Your R-number lives in our system. Future orders take minutes — log in to the portal or call us directly.
